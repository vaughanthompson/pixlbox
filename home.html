A closer look at Pixlbox

Below are web design and development requirements we regularly provide for our customers.

  • E-Commerce functionality. Create products, manage inventory, process payments, and handle shipping and taxes.
  • Security. Measures to protect the website and users' data from unauthorized access, hacking, and other types of cyberattacks.
  • User authentication & authorization. Creating user accounts, logging in and out, and managing user roles and permissions.
  • Responsive design. Ensuring that the website not only looks good on desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones- but that content is tailored to each use case (UX).
  • Content management. Tools for users to easily create, edit, and delete website content, such as blog posts, product content, and pages.
  • Social media integrations. Allowing visitors to share content on social media platforms and to log in to the website using their social media accounts.
  • Analytics & tracking. This tracks user behavior and provides metrics such as website traffic, conversion rates, and user demographics.
  • Scalability. The ability of the website to handle increasing traffic and data without crashing or slowing down.
