Pixlbox Services

At Pixlbox we offer a wide range of design and development services.

The beauty of being a Pixlbox client is knowing that you can come to us for tech, creative or print services as and when you need them.

Development

We program to the highest standards using the latest, stable Microsoft products. Our services are continually evolving as technology advances in security, performance and features.

Even if you can't see it on the list below, there's a very high chance whatever it is you need developed - we can do it.


  • E-Commerce, Catalogue, Brochure and Brand-based (CMS) websites
  • Penetration testing / secure sites
  • Custom web-app development
  • API creation / integration
  • Database design
  • Programming and scripting
  • Front-end coding (HTML5/CSS3/Javascript)
  • Site / web-app architecture and specification design
  • Native app development (contract)

Design for tech

Pixlbox design usually starts at this mid point between tech and creative. Often it works so well the client wishes to see it go beyond the screen into the "real world".

This is why we strive to ensure any design produced is both applicable in our development environment and extendable for any brand requirements.


  • User experience design / wireframing and user journey
  • Web and app prototyping
  • Responsive design and UI layout (Web)
  • Native app design and assets production
  • Logomark design, Typography, Iconography and vector illustration (SVG)
  • Organic & Paid SEO (contract)

Creative

With a eye for detail, a modern, minimal design sense and a sharp tone of voice, we not only build out creative for online- we can also produce collateral for print, large format or other media.

Additionally we can extend a digital idea into a wider campaign creative or brand identity.


  • Brand / Visual Identity & Guidelines
  • Tactical creative for web & print
  • Print, Large format & environmental
  • Packaging, Vehicle livery & apparel
  • Marketing copywriting
  • Image (Visual) composition
  • Photography (contract)
