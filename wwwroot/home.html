About

Pixlbox is a digital studio based in Christchurch, New Zealand.


We are a small, remote working team building websites and web-based apps for clients across New Zealand and worldwide. With proven experience in both design and development, Pixlbox offers a surprisingly wide range of services.

Unlike bigger agency teams, we are lean and agile. As the people who actually build your tech, we develop our websites and apps in collaboration with our clients. Without the filtering of communication and jargon that often comes with larger digital marketing agencies who contract out development- you'll get straight talking, then and there.


We make

E-Commerce websites. Online catalogues. Brand microsites. Brochure websites. Web based apps. Databases. Programming. Prototypes and MVPs. Content driven (CMS) websites. Intranets and private web apps. Third party (API) integrations.


We create

Web/App UX (User experience) design. Web/App UI (User interface) design. Brand identity and design systems. Brand positioning and guidelines. Logo development. Print collateral. Large format (billboard & signage). Packaging. Iconography.
